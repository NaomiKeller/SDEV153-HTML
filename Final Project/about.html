About the Author

Hello! My character name is Tepes, SEGA ID is moonkitty. I play on Ship 2, as it has the largest English speaking player base. I have been playing PSO2 for 3 years now. It's a game I can't get enough of, and always like to learn new things, meet new people, and keep going for the best gear.

I have been in a few teams, but am really happy with the team I just settled into called Starwind. My main class at the moment is Hero, and I am one of the top 3 DPS in the team. Our team is very active and accepting to new members and new players. We run Emergency Quests together frequently.

I set out to create this website because PSO2 is a very hard game to get into, especially without any resources to guide you along. If it wasn't for dozens of helpful people and resources, it would have taken me ages to understand all of the aspects of the game. Even still, I learn new things about the game every day. This website's purpose is to help those who were in the same position as me when I first started to get on there feet and progress through the game.
